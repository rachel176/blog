My Life with Dogs

My two teddy bears, Ein and Dew, are super adorable, silly, and sassy. Here you will find pictures and anecdotes about our adventures with them!

Ein is the oldest pup that we have, and we took a lot of pictures of him as a baby, especially silly ones where he is playing or sleeping.

Ein was definitely more of a dog-dog, so we got him a friend. We named his buddy, Dew.

Overall, we have some pretty spoiled pups... They love being by us and watching outside, which is pretty easy to do with our home setup.

Both dogs are unique in their own way... Ein needed treats to learn how to walk away from home while Dew was thrilled to go on a run with me. Here's Ein walking after some training and after my first successful outdoor run with Dew!
